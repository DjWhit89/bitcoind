\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Sort eviction candidates by network/localhost and connection uptime. Candidates near the beginning are more likely to be evicted, and those near the end are more likely to be protected, e.\+g. less likely to be evicted.
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item First, nodes that are not {\ttfamily is\+\_\+local} and that do not belong to {\ttfamily network}, sorted by increasing uptime (from most recently connected to connected longer).
\item Then, nodes that are {\ttfamily is\+\_\+local} or belong to {\ttfamily network}, sorted by increasing uptime. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
