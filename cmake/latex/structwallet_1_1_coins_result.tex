\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
COutputs available for spending, stored by \doxyref{Output\+Type}{p.}{outputtype_8h_a4e0517338e6c4a31a2addafc06d4f3a3}. This struct is really just a wrapper around \doxyref{Output\+Type}{p.}{outputtype_8h_a4e0517338e6c4a31a2addafc06d4f3a3} vectors with a convenient method for concatenating and returning all COutputs as one vector.
